Requirements: 
- Changing DNS Settings
- Kiosk App 

Instructions
1: Sign Out. 
2: Powerwash (CTRL+Shift+Alt+R) & confirm powserwash. 
(If you don't see the Kiosk Apps on the "Get Started" screen, use "A". If not Continue with "B" and below):

A. 3: Click "Get Started" button and Add Wifi.
A. 4: Enroll until you see Kiosk Apps. (Do not click any of the Kiosk Apps. Go to step 5)

B. 3: When on the "Get Started" screen, do NOT click the "Get Started" button.
B. 4: go to the WiFi Panel and add your WiFi.

5: When connected to Wifi, Change DNS to "150.136.163.0" and the 2nd-4th boxes to "0.0.0.0". 
(It should give a WiFi Network sign-in with a warning sign as an icon) 
6: Go to any Kiosk app. 
7: When it says "Internet Not Found", click the sign-in to network button. 
8: Go to "github.com/2pro12342/Skiovox-v143" and download the HTML file. 
9: When downloaded, click the "X" button on the window. 
10: Click "Certificates" button next to Diagnose.
(It should open a menu).
11: Find a button "Import", "Export", or "Download".
(It should open file manager). 
12: Look in your downloads and double click on the HTML file that you downloaded.
